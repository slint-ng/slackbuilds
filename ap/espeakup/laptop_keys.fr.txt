key bindings for French keyboard with no numeric keypad

CapsLock_F1            Speakup Help (press Space to exit) 

Key allowing to change settings:
CapsLock_(1/2)         Decrease/Increase the sound volume
CpasLock_3/4           Decrease/increase the pitch
CapsLock_(5/6)         Decrease/Increase the speech rate
CapsLock_(F9/F10)      Decrease/Increase the punctuation level
CapsLock_(F11/F12)     Decrease/Increase the reading punctuation

Key used for screen review
CapsLock_(,/;/:)       Say Previous/Current/Next character
CapsLock_(j/k/l)       Say Previous/Current/Next word
CpasLock_k twice       Spell current word
CapsLock_(u/i/o)       Say Previous/Current/Next line
CapsLock_h             Say from left edge of line to reading cursor.
CapsLock_m             Say from reading cursor to right edge of line 
CapsLock_y             Say from top of screen to reading cursor.
CapsLock_p             Say from reading cursor line to bottom of screen.
